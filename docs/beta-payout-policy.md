# Beta Payout Policy

## Overview

During the Beta phase, GetFanSee operates with a simplified payout system. This document outlines the current payout rules, requirements, and processes.

## Beta Phase Limitations

During Beta, the following limitations apply:

| Feature                 | Status        | Notes                      |
| ----------------------- | ------------- | -------------------------- |
| Automated payouts       | Not available | Manual processing only     |
| Instant withdrawals     | Not available | 7-14 day processing        |
| Multiple payout methods | Limited       | Bank transfer only (US)    |
| KYC verification        | Placeholder   | Will be required at launch |

## Payout Rules

### Minimum Withdrawal Amount

| Currency | Minimum Amount |
| -------- | -------------- |
| USD      | $50.00         |

**Rationale**: Minimum threshold reduces transaction costs and processing overhead during manual phase.

### Platform Fees

| Fee Type           | Percentage   |
| ------------------ | ------------ |
| Platform fee       | 20%          |
| Payment processing | ~3% (varies) |

**Example Calculation**:

- Creator earnings: $100.00
- Platform fee (20%): -$20.00
- Net to creator: $80.00
- Payment processing (~3%): -$2.40
- Final payout: ~$77.60

### Payout Schedule

During Beta:

- Payouts processed: **Weekly** (every Friday)
- Processing time: **7-14 business days**
- Cutoff time: Wednesday 11:59 PM UTC

## Withdrawal Process (Beta)

### Step 1: Request Withdrawal

1. Navigate to Creator Studio > Earnings
2. Click "Request Withdrawal"
3. Enter amount (minimum $50.00)
4. Confirm payout details

### Step 2: Verification (Placeholder)

Currently disabled during Beta. At launch:

- Identity verification (KYC)
- Tax information (W-9 for US creators)
- Bank account verification

### Step 3: Processing

Manual processing by GetFanSee team:

1. Request reviewed within 48 hours
2. Funds transferred via bank wire
3. Email confirmation sent

### Step 4: Receipt

- Funds arrive in 7-14 business days
- Transaction history updated in dashboard

## Eligibility Requirements

To request a payout, creators must:

1. **Account in good standing** - No policy violations
2. **Minimum balance** - At least $50.00 available
3. **Valid payout method** - Bank account on file
4. **Complete profile** - All required fields filled

### Currently Available

### Coming Soon (Post-Beta)

## Tax Considerations

### US Creators

- 1099-K issued for earnings > $600/year
- Tax ID (SSN/EIN) required at launch
- Platform reports to IRS

### International Creators

- Tax withholding may apply
- W-8BEN form required
- Local tax obligations apply

## Dispute Resolution

### Common Issues

| Issue            | Resolution                          |
| ---------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Missing payout   | Contact support with transaction ID |
| Incorrect amount | Review fee breakdown in dashboard   |
| Delayed transfer | Check bank processing times         |

### Contact Support

- Email: payouts@getfansee.com
- Response time: 24-48 hours
- Include: Username, transaction ID, issue description

## Important Notices

### No Refunds on Payouts

Once a payout is processed, it cannot be reversed. Ensure:

- Correct bank details on file
- Sufficient balance before requesting
- Understanding of applicable fees

### Account Closure

If you close your account:

- Pending payouts will be processed
- Final payout within 30 days
- Minimum balance rules still apply

### Policy Changes

GetFanSee reserves the right to:

- Modify payout rules with notice
- Adjust fees during Beta
- Require additional verification

## FAQ

**Q: Why is there a minimum payout?**
A: Minimum thresholds reduce processing costs and ensure sustainable operations during Beta.

**Q: When will automated payouts be available?**
A: Planned for post-Beta launch, with Stripe Connect integration.

**Q: Can I change my payout method?**
A: Yes, update in Settings > Payout Methods. Changes take effect next payout cycle.

**Q: What happens to earnings below minimum?**
A: Balance accumulates until minimum is reached. No expiration on earned balance.

**Q: Are there any holdback periods?**
A: Yes, 7-day holdback on new earnings to allow for refund/chargeback processing.
